how the game works:

  • easy takes 500 milliseconds to show the number, medium takes 250 milliseconds and hard takes 50 milliseconds
  • you earn 10 points in easy difficulty, 15 points in medium and 30 in hard
  • however, in hard mode, if you get a number above 100 (or especially above 175) you will get more points (40-50)
  • easy difficulty's maximum clicks required is 50, medium is 100 and hard is 250
